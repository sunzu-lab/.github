# Sun Zu Lab

## Transparency is the foundation of trust

Web3 market making has operated in the dark for too long. Token issuers sign agreements they cannot verify. Performance data is controlled by the counterparty. And when things go wrong, there is no independent record to turn to.

Sun Zu Lab exists to change that. We built an independent platform that gives token issuers full visibility into their liquidity operations – the data, the analytics, the contract monitoring, and the AI-powered tools to act on it all.

We believe that better information leads to better decisions, and better decisions lead to healthier markets for everyone.
